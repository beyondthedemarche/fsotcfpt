Job Knowledge
Which U.S. government agency is primarily responsible for managing foreign affairs and diplomatic relations?
A.Department of Homeland Security
B.Department of Defense
C.Department of State
D.Department of Justice
The Department of State manages U.S. foreign affairs and diplomacy, including maintaining embassies and consulates, issuing passports and visas, and representing the U.S. in international negotiations. DHS handles immigration enforcement and border security; the Department of Defense oversees the military.
English Expression
The officer noted that the applicant had [went] to the embassy twice without success.

For the underlined word, which is correct?
A.went
B.gone
C.go
D.goes
The past perfect construction "had gone" requires the past participle "gone," not the simple past "went." The verb "had" signals that a past participle must follow. "Had went" is always incorrect in standard English.
Logical Reasoning
All consular officers must complete training before being posted abroad. Maria has been posted abroad.

Which conclusion follows logically?
A.Maria has not completed training.
B.Maria completed training before being posted.
C.Maria was exempt from training requirements.
D.Maria chose to complete training voluntarily.
This is a straightforward deductive syllogism. The first premise states that ALL officers must complete training before posting. Maria has been posted. Therefore Maria must have completed training - this follows necessarily from the premises. Options A, C, and D either contradict the premise or introduce unsupported assumptions.
